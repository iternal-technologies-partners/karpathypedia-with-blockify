The **SME Training service** is an offering designed to develop the capabilities of internal teams within an organization. It focuses specifically on equipping staff with the knowledge and skills necessary to carry out block review and governance responsibilities. This service represents a structured approach to building internal expertise in these specialized areas.

## Overview

The SME Training service is oriented toward empowering internal teams by providing them with targeted instruction in block review and governance. By focusing on these two core disciplines, the service ensures that team members are prepared to handle responsibilities that are central to maintaining organizational standards and oversight. The training is designed for internal audiences, meaning it is intended to build capacity within an organization rather than serving external clients or stakeholders.

## Core Components

### Block Review Training

A foundational element of the SME Training service is instruction in block review. This component prepares internal team members to evaluate, assess, and work with blocks in a systematic and informed manner. By developing competency in block review, participants gain the ability to contribute meaningfully to quality assurance and content oversight processes within their organization.

### Governance Training

Alongside block review, governance forms the second principal component of the SME Training service. Governance training equips internal teams with an understanding of the frameworks, processes, and responsibilities that guide decision-making and accountability. This aspect of the service ensures that team members are not only technically capable but also understand the broader organizational context in which their work takes place.

## Target Audience

The SME Training service is directed at internal teams. This focus on internal staff distinguishes the service as one concerned with organizational capacity building. By targeting those who already work within the organization, the service aims to cultivate subject matter expertise from within, reducing reliance on external guidance and fostering a more self-sufficient workforce in the areas of block review and governance.

## Purpose and Value

The primary purpose of the SME Training service is to develop and strengthen the competencies of internal teams in two critical areas: block review and governance. Organizations that engage with this service benefit from having staff who are better prepared to fulfill oversight and review responsibilities. The cultivation of internal expertise in these domains can contribute to more consistent and informed decision-making, as well as a stronger overall governance posture.

By investing in the training of internal personnel, organizations signal a commitment to building durable, in-house capabilities rather than depending solely on external resources for these functions. The SME Training service thus serves as a mechanism for knowledge transfer and skill development that can have lasting organizational impact.
